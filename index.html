Who Are We

A-Feed is the result of the collective effort of Bangkit Academy's student cohort specializing in machine learning, cloud computing, and mobile development. As part of our capstone project, we have come together to tackle the challenges in fish farming by developing innovative automatic feeding solutions.

What We Do

At A-Feed, we offer automatic feeding solutions for fish farming. Our technology combines IoT and machine learning to provide optimal feeding schedules, improving fish health and farm efficiency.
